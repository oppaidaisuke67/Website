Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to Settings → Account and click "Update" next to your password. You'll receive a verification email to set a new password. Alternatively, use the "Forgot password" link on the login page.

How can I plan a trip with friends?

Navigate to the Plans tab, build your itinerary, and then use the "Share with friends" option inside the plan. Your friends can comment and suggest changes.

What are the subscription plans?

We offer a free Basic plan with core features. Premium plans (Explorer, Wanderer) unlock offline maps, priority support, and exclusive travel guides. Check Settings → Subscription for details.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the flag icon (🟨) on any post or comment. Our moderation team will review it within 24 hours. You can also email support@guidetojapan.com directly.

How do I cancel my account?

Visit Settings → Account → Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm your decision. Data deletion is permanent after 30 days of inactivity.
